Entry / Exit gate
Common — all facilities
Vehicle ANPR
Auto-read number plates of trucks, vans, and cars. Log entry/exit times, verify expected deliveries.
Automated boom barrier
Trigger barrier open/close based on verified plate or RFID — no manual guard action required.
Vehicle type classification
Distinguish heavy goods vehicles from light vans, cars, motorcycles — route to correct bays automatically.
Unauthorised vehicle alert
Flag plates not on the approved list in real time. Push alert to security and log event with timestamp.
Worker entry / locker area
Common — all facilities
Uniform & PPE compliance
Detect whether workers are wearing correct uniforms, hard hats, hi-vis vests, gloves before entering the floor.
Headcount & shift tracking
Count workers entering and exiting each zone. Cross-reference against shift rosters and alert on discrepancies.
Crowding & zone density
Monitor locker room and choke-point density — alert supervisors when crowds exceed safe thresholds.
Access zone enforcement
Detect workers entering restricted zones without authorisation. Real-time alert and audit trail.
Canteen / break area
Common — all facilities
Hand hygiene monitoring
Verify workers wash hands at designated stations before returning to the floor. Log compliance for audits.
Crowding & queue alerts
Detect overcrowding at serving counters or exit points. Alert facility management to redistribute flow.
Tray / utensil tracking
Count trays and detect items left behind or dropped — useful in high-throughput canteen environments.
Break time compliance
Detect workers still in break areas past permitted times. Passive alert, no confrontation required.
Security / control room
Common — all facilities
Perimeter intrusion detection
AI models on existing CCTV detect fence breaches, loitering near boundaries, or movement in off-hours.
Behaviour anomaly alerts
Detect running, falling, fighting, or unusual movement patterns — alert guard team in real time.
Consolidated camera dashboard
AI-ranked alert feed across all cameras — no more watching 40 feeds. Guards see only flagged clips.
Incident clip extraction
Auto-clip relevant footage around any alert, timestamped and ready for insurance or regulatory reporting.
Goods in / dispatch dock
Common — all facilities
Item counting & reconciliation
Count units entering or leaving on pallets or conveyors. Reconcile against purchase orders automatically.
Barcode / QR / label OCR
Read labels, batch numbers, and SKUs at belt speed. Push directly to WMS or ERP — no manual scanning.
Damage detection on arrival
Flag damaged packaging, dented goods, or broken seals at the point of receipt. Photo evidence auto-generated.
Truck load verification
Verify loaded pallets match dispatch manifest before truck departure. Reduce mis-shipment and customer claims.
Warehouse / storage
Common — all facilities
Shelf occupancy monitoring
Overhead or aisle cameras detect empty bays and low-stock slots — trigger replenishment alerts automatically.
Pick accuracy verification
Vision confirms the correct SKU and quantity is picked before it leaves the station — reduces returns.
Forklift & pedestrian safety
Detect when pedestrians enter forklift operating zones. Alert driver and floor supervisor in real time.
Pallet dimension scan
3D vision measures pallet dimensions and weight distribution before placement — avoids racking overload.
Cleaning / maintenance activity
Common — all facilities
Cleaning activity detection
Verify cleaning crews have actually cleaned zones — detect mop, bucket, and movement patterns. Auto log.
Missed area alerts
Compare pre- and post-cleaning camera frames. Flag zones that were not cleaned within the expected window.
Maintenance task verification
Confirm engineers have attended to flagged equipment. Timestamp-verified record for ISO and safety audits.
Spillage detection
Detect liquid spills or debris on the floor in real time — alert cleaning team before a slip hazard develops.
Production floor
Industry-specific
Weld & surface defect detection
AI vision inspects welds, body panels, and castings at line speed — catching flaws invisible to the naked eye.
Dimensional & tolerance check
3D cameras measure against CAD specs in real time. Flags out-of-tolerance parts before assembly.
Torque & fastener verification
Confirm all bolts are present and correctly seated after each assembly station — before the part moves on.
Ergonomic risk monitoring
Detect repetitive strain postures at assembly stations. Flag for workstation redesign before injury occurs.
Label & expiry verification
OCR reads every label, expiry date, and lot number at line speed. Prevents recall-triggering mislabelling.
Fill-level & seal integrity
Vision checks fill volumes and cap/foil seal status on every unit — flagging deviations before packing.
Foreign particle detection
Combined with X-ray or IR, detects contaminants in transparent and opaque packaging. EU GMP ready.
Gowning & hygiene compliance
Verify cleanroom gowning is complete before entry. Log compliance for regulatory audits automatically.
Parcel & pallet dimensioning
3D vision captures length, width, height, and weight — feeds billing and routing systems without manual measurement.
Sortation accuracy
Cameras verify each parcel goes to the correct chute or lane — catches wrong-sort events before dispatch.
Damage detection at sort
Identify damaged parcels before they leave the facility — generate photo evidence and route to claims.
Throughput & bottleneck analytics
Track parcel flow across the belt in real time. Identify where the line slows and when — connected to WMS.
PCB & solder inspection (AOI)
AI-enhanced AOI detects solder bridges, missing components, and misalignments at higher speed and accuracy.
Component presence & orientation
Confirm every SMD component is present, correctly seated, and polarised before reflow — zero false passes.
Connector & cable verification
Vision checks connector seating depth and cable routing — catches assembly errors invisible to line operators.
Serial & marking verification
Read laser-engraved serials, barcodes, and QR codes on every board — reconcile against MES records.
Surface crack & scratch detection
Vision at micron resolution detects cracks, scratches, and pitting on machined metal surfaces and cast parts.
Tool wear monitoring
Cameras and thermal sensors track cutting-tool wear — trigger replacement before quality degrades.
Chip & swarf detection
Detect metal swarf build-up around machines — trigger cleaning before it causes jams or safety incidents.
Hot work & spark monitoring
Detect welding and grinding sparks leaving safe zones — real-time fire risk alert connected to EHS system.
